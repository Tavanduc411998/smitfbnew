Ads check by SMIT

The software supports checking and managing Facebook advertising accounts. With many useful features, Ads Check by SMIT helps to save time, optimize resources and maximize the efficiency of ad account management.

Main Features

  • 1. Ads Check Pro

    Quickly view all Ads Account's indicators with just one click: Balance, Threshold, Hidden limit, ID,...and also information about BM accounts which you own.

  • 2. Super Share Ads

    Share the ability to manage numerous accounts simultaneously with a variety of various permission choices, and rename accounts after sharing for simple administration.

  • 3. Share Pixel

    Share the ability to manage numerous accounts simultaneously with a variety of various permission choices, and rename accounts after sharing for simple administration.

  • 4. Remove Hidden Admins

    Check and remove hidden Admins located in your Ads assets easily.

  • 5. Extended Payment

    Gives users an easy way to monitor Ads Account's information by displaying account's information directly on the Settings page and by providing an information bubble feature on the Ads Manager page.

  • 6. Change Ads Account's info

    Change Time Zone, Currency, ID, Business Information that owns a large number of advertising accounts at the same time, instead of having to manipulate each account.

  • 7. Super Target

    Taken from Facebook's target library, allowing advertisers to add more choices than the 25 interests that Ads Manager is suggesting.

II. Ads check by SMIT can help you

  • 1. Save time with Ads Check Pro

    Too many actions require time:

    Own numerous accounts but are unable to view all data at once; instead, you must visit the website for each individual account. This is what causes advertisers to take too long.

    SMIT's solution

    Instead of going to each website and each account, you can manage all of your active Ads Accounts and easily examine your account's stats on a separate page. checking the financial statement signs is unnecessary and time-consuming.

    Restrict ads...Your Ads Account still works but has limited operations to advertise, so sharing permissions for other accounts is very necessary to continue to use, but this operation on Ads Manager takes a lot of time and manipulation.

    With Super Share Ads, with just a few simple steps, you can already share administrative rights for many accounts at the same time, thereby optimizing time and preventing risks of limited accounts.
